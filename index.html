Welcome to the Help page of my Minecraft Bot. You can find a command list, a change log and more information here.

Commands

Info

  • help Get this help menu.
  • ping [player name] Gives you your or someone else’s ping.
  • uuid [player name] Find a Minecraft player’s UUID.
  • joindate [player name] Get you or someone elses join date. This command is limited by the bot’s data.

Admin

  • echo (text) Repeat the text you say.
  • tpa [player name] Send a TPA to you or another player.
  • kill Kill the bot.
