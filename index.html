Anarchy Bot

Welcome to the Help page of my Minecraft Bot. You can find a command list, a change log and more information here.

Commands

Info

  • help: Shows this help file.
  • ping: Shows your or someone else’s ping.
  • oldnames || on: shows the last 3 names from someone.
  • skin: sends you the link to someone’s skin.
  • bp: shows the player with the best ping.
  • wp: shows the player with the worst ping.
  • sleep: tells you if you can sleep or not.
  • rules: no. NO!
  • namemc || nm: shows the link to someone’s NameMC profile.

Admin

  • echo: repeats your text.
  • tpto: send a TPA to someone.
  • big: OwO big text!
  • kill: /kill!

Misc

  • fact: shows you a random fact. You can also do fact de for a German fact. API
  • urban: gives you the Urban Definiton of a word.
  • yes: YES!
  • no: guess what…
  • queue: gives you the 2b2t queue length.
  • 2bseen: last seen for 2b2t.
  • 2bstates: shows you the kills and deaths from a player on 2b2t.
